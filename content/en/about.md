NORDNOVA is a Swedish consultancy specialising in architectural design, structural engineering, energy-efficient buildings, interior design, and international trade in building materials and steel products.

The firm was founded by Sam Abrahamsson — a construction engineer with a background spanning Sweden, Germany, Malaysia, and Sudan — with the ambition to bring Scandinavian precision and global perspective to every project.

We believe that the best buildings arise from the intersection of technical excellence and thoughtful design. Whether we are drawing up a building permit application in Småland, optimising the energy performance of a residential build, or connecting a steel manufacturer in Asia with a client in the Middle East, we bring the same commitment to quality, clarity, and trust.

NORDNOVA is based in Hultsfred, Sweden, and works across the Nordic region and internationally.
